About Us


So, you might be wondering why this person would want to sell haunted houses. You might think that there are a couple of screws loose. Well, that might be true, but when this project started, it was after discovering that there is a market and a desire for haunted houses. Along with our estate agents, we work closely with our clients to ensure we deliver a home suited to their needs.

With our dedication, innovation and commitment to providing the best customer service, we have been able to stay on top of the Haunted Property Industry.

Starting off in Eastern Europe, we have now been able to expand our business to Western Europe. With continued growth, we do not plan on ever moving away from our culture and objectives.
